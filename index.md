Hello! My name is Brandon Park Coy and I'm a third year PhD student at the University of Chicago. My research interests lie in the habitability and atmospheric composition of small extrasolar planets (exoplanets), as well as the atmosphere of Titan.  

I am currently advised by Geophysical Sciences Professor Edwin Kite and Astronomy & Astrophysics Professor [Eliza Kempton](https://astrophysics.uchicago.edu/people/profile/eliza-kempton/).
